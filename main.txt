# Kitchen Brain (KB) is an application.

You can interact with KB via an LLM using English.

## Example conversations

prompt 1:

here's a few dishes / recipes / restaurants / cuisines that i like:
asian food in general, fajitas, pizza.  I don't like casseroles or
lasagna.

and here's what's in my pantry: (list of ingredients follows, like
potatoes, typical american condiments, etc).

here's my calorie and nutrient daily goals: about 2500 calories, and
about 100 grams of protein daily, and standard USRDA stuff for fiber,
vitamins, etc.

with all that in mind, what can i make for dinner?

prompt 2:

record that i just bought these groceries (maybe i upload an image of a
receipt or provide a saved email receipt).

prompt 3:

record that i ate this meal (for example, four slices of cheese pizza)
and keep a database of my calories and nutrients, sort of like
myfitnesspal or any other calorie-counting app.

prompt 4:

make a menu plan for the week based on what I already have in my
kitchen when possible.  Make me a grocery list of required ingredients
that I don't already have.
